- Developed compelling NPCs with distinct personalities, motivations, and backgrounds
- Designed plot hooks and story arcs keeping participants engaged across multiple sessions
- Improvised narrative elements responding to unexpected player choices and actions
- Incorporated participant character backgrounds into main storyline creating personal investment

**Customization & Participant Engagement:**
- Listened to participant preferences adapting storylines to match group interests
- Integrated player character backstories into campaign narrative creating meaningful connections
- Balanced spotlight time ensuring all participants had moments to shine
- Adjusted complexity and themes appropriate for mixed age group (adults and teens)

**Safe Environment & Conflict Mediation:**
- Upheld safe, collaborative environment where all participants felt comfortable and respected

- Mediated interpersonal conflicts between participants using conflict resolution strategies
